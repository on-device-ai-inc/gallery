OnDevice AI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

🔐 Privacy-First: OnDevice AI processes all AI operations locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your AI inputs, outputs, or personal data.

1. What We Collect at Purchase

When you purchase OnDevice AI, we collect your email address to deliver your download link and send a receipt. Payment details are handled entirely by Stripe (card) or Safaricom M-Pesa (mobile money) — we never see or store your card number or M-Pesa PIN.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • AI inputs or outputs (conversations stay on your device)
  • Location data
  • Photos or media
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond your purchase email

3. On-Device Processing

All AI processing happens entirely on your device. No internet is required for AI features. Your data never leaves your device.

4. Analytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports so we can fix bugs. No conversation content, prompts, or personal information is included in crash reports.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for card payments and Safaricom M-Pesa for mobile payments in Kenya. We use Resend to deliver transactional email. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

OnDevice AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
