# ChatGPT Charges $5 Every Month Forever. We Charged $3.99 Once. Here's the Difference.

**6 min read**

---

In October 2025, OpenAI launched **ChatGPT Go** in Kenya at $5 per month. This was notable for two reasons: it was a deliberate move into African markets, and it confirmed that OpenAI sees the region as a viable revenue base for AI subscriptions.

We saw the announcement and thought about it seriously. Not because we fear the competition — but because it told us something important: **the market is real, the demand is validated, and now the question is what kind of product serves it best.**

Our answer is $3.99. Once. No subscription.

Here is an honest breakdown of what that means.

---

## What ChatGPT Go Actually Gives You

ChatGPT Go is a solid product. Let's be clear about that.

For $5 a month, you get:

- **Access to GPT-4o**, one of the most capable language models available today
- **Internet-connected responses** — the model can browse the web and return current information
- **Multimodal input** — you can send images, voice notes, and documents
- **OpenAI's infrastructure** — fast, globally distributed, with high availability
- **Continuous model improvements** — your subscription covers access to upgrades as they happen

These are real advantages. GPT-4o is, by most benchmarks, more capable than Gemma for complex reasoning, nuanced analysis, and tasks requiring broad world knowledge. If you are a professional doing demanding cognitive work and you can comfortably absorb the cost, ChatGPT Go is a strong product at a reasonable price for what it delivers.

We are not in the business of pretending otherwise.

## The Honest Tradeoffs

This is where we are going to be straight with you.

**GPT-4o is a more capable model than Gemma.** If you need to analyze a complex legal document, write advanced academic research, or complete tasks requiring deep multi-step reasoning over long contexts, GPT-4o will outperform Gemma. That is simply true and we are not going to pretend otherwise.

**ChatGPT can browse the internet. OnDevice AI cannot.** If you need current news, live prices, or real-time information, ChatGPT Go has a structural advantage that on-device inference cannot match by design.

**ChatGPT Go works on any device with a browser. OnDevice AI requires Android 9+ with 3GB+ RAM.** If you switch between devices frequently or primarily use a low-end phone, that matters.

These are the honest tradeoffs. We are not building the same product. We are building a different product with different priorities.

## Who Should Choose OnDevice AI

**Students with limited data plans.** If you are a university student in Lagos, Nairobi, or Cape Town managing a 5GB monthly bundle, the zero-data-cost model is not a minor benefit — it is the entire value proposition. Fifty AI conversations at zero marginal data cost is qualitatively different from fifty conversations that eat into your bundle.

**Privacy-conscious users.** Not everyone wants their questions logged on a server in California. Medical questions, financial planning, personal journaling, legal research — there are categories of query that feel meaningfully different when you know they are processed entirely on-device and never transmitted. OnDevice AI's privacy guarantee is structural, not policy-based. It is not "we promise not to look at your data." It is "your data never reaches us."

**Anyone tired of subscriptions.** There is a real cognitive and financial cost to managing a portfolio of recurring SaaS charges. Every subscription is a commitment that requires monitoring, cancellation management, and budget allocation. One-time purchases are simpler. You buy the thing, you own the thing, you use the thing.

**Developers and builders in Africa.** For a developer in Nairobi or Lagos using AI as a daily coding assistant, the combination of zero data cost and offline availability turns AI from a premium tool into ambient infrastructure. It is always there, always fast, costs nothing per conversation.

**Users in areas with inconsistent connectivity.** Matatus, upcountry travel, load-shedding periods, office buildings with poor mobile signal — on-device AI works everywhere your phone works, independent of network conditions.

## Who Should Choose ChatGPT Go

We will say it plainly:

If you do demanding professional work that genuinely benefits from frontier model reasoning — you are an analyst, a researcher, a lawyer, a complex writer — and you can comfortably absorb $5/month, ChatGPT Go delivers more raw capability per conversation.

If you need internet-connected AI — current news, live data, web research integrated into responses — ChatGPT Go is built for that use case in a way on-device inference cannot replicate.

If you primarily use AI on a desktop browser rather than a phone, ChatGPT's interface is more mature for that context.

We are not trying to convince everyone to switch. We are trying to be clear about which product is right for which user.
