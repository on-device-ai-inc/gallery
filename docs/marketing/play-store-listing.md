## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**Your AI. Your Device. Your Privacy.**

OnDevice AI runs advanced AI language models entirely on your phone — no internet required, no data sent to servers, no subscriptions. Your conversations stay on your device, always.

**🔒 100% Private**
Everything happens locally. Your chats, your images, your voice — none of it ever leaves your device. No accounts required to use AI.

**💬 Chat with AI**
Have natural conversations with state-of-the-art language models including Gemma 3, Qwen 2.5, and DeepSeek. Ask anything, get instant responses — even in airplane mode.

**🖼️ Generate Images**
Create images from text descriptions using Stable Diffusion, running entirely on your device's GPU.

**🎙️ Voice Understanding**
Ask questions using your voice. The app processes audio locally — your recordings never leave your phone.

**📷 Visual AI**
Point your camera at anything and ask questions about what you see. All vision processing is on-device.

**⚡ Optimized for Android**
Built with Google's MediaPipe and LiteRT frameworks for maximum performance on Android hardware. Supports GPU acceleration for faster responses.

**🌐 Web Search (Optional)**
Optionally connect to the web for up-to-date information while keeping your AI conversations private.

**Open Source Foundation**
Built on the Google AI Edge Gallery (Apache 2.0). Full attributions available in Settings → Legal Information.

---

**Supported Models:**
• Gemma 3 1B — Fast, efficient, great for everyday tasks
• Gemma 3n E2B / E4B — Multimodal, supports images
• Qwen 2.5 1.5B — Multilingual conversations
• DeepSeek R1 Distill — Advanced reasoning
• Stable Diffusion 1.5 — Image generation

**Requirements:**
• Android 12 or higher (API 31+)
• 6GB+ RAM recommended
• 4–8GB free storage for models
