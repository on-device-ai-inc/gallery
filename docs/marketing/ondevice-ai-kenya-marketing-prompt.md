# AI Prompt: Create Marketing Plan for OnDevice AI - Kenya Launch

You are a senior marketing strategist specializing in emerging markets, mobile apps, and dual-market business models (premium users funding free access for underserved communities). Create a comprehensive go-to-market strategy for OnDevice AI launching in Kenya.

---

## PRODUCT CONTEXT

**Product Name:** OnDevice AI

**What It Is:**
OnDevice AI is a mobile AI assistant (like ChatGPT) that runs 100% on-device. All AI processing happens on the user's smartphone—no internet required after initial model download, zero data transmitted to cloud servers.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- **Privacy-Conscious Users:** "Your AI, Your Data, Your Device. ChatGPT-level AI without surveillance or cloud dependency."
- **Kenya/Emerging Markets:** "AI for Everyone. Works offline. Free forever. No data charges."

**Key Differentiators vs Cloud AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude):**
1. **100% Offline**: Download AI model once (600MB-4GB), use forever without internet
2. **100% Private**: All processing on-device, architecturally impossible to transmit data
3. **Free Tier**: Full AI access at no cost (ad-free, no limitations)
4. **Low Cost Premium**: $2-5/month in Kenya (vs $20/month for ChatGPT Plus)
5. **Mission-Driven**: Premium subscriptions fund free access for students and underserved communities

**Technical Specs:**
- Platform: Android (7.0+), iOS planned
- Model Size: 600MB (basic) to 4GB (advanced)
- App Size: <30MB
- Languages: English, Swahili (more coming)
- Multimodal: Text, voice input, image understanding
- Performance: Response starts <1 second, 20-45 tokens/second

**Current Status:**
- Version: 1.1.9 (Build 35)
- Stage: Pre-launch (MVP at ~35% completion)
- Focus: Kenya + Zimbabwe as primary emerging markets, privacy-conscious users globally

## KENYA MARKET CONTEXT

**Why Kenya?**
1. **M-Pesa Ecosystem**: 50M+ active users, world's most advanced mobile money infrastructure
2. **Connectivity Challenges**: 3G/4G unreliable in rural areas, data costs $6-12 per GB
3. **Tech-Savvy Population**: High smartphone adoption, early adopter culture
4. **Youth Demographics**: 75% under age 35, hungry for education and economic opportunity
5. **Language**: English + Swahili official languages (reduces localization complexity)

**Market Size:**
- Total Addressable Market: 28M smartphone users in Kenya
- Serviceable Addressable Market: 15M users with 2GB+ RAM devices
- Target Year 1: 500K-1M users (3-6% of SAM)

**Key Consumer Segments:**
1. **Students & Educators (40%)**: Homework help, exam prep, can't afford ChatGPT
2. **Small Business Owners (25%)**: Need AI for business content, emails, ideas
3. **Tech-Savvy Professionals (20%)**: Privacy-conscious, coding/productivity use
4. **Rural Communities (15%)**: Unreliable internet, agriculture/health advice

**Competitive Landscape:**
- **ChatGPT**: $20/mo (KSh 2,600), requires internet, privacy concerns
- **Google Gemini**: Free but requires internet, Google privacy issues
- **Tutoring Services**: Education-specific (Zeraki, Eneza), subscription-based
- **Data Costs**: Users spend KSh 500-2,000/month on data for online AI

## PRICING STRATEGY (KENYA)

**FREE TIER (Target: 80% of users)**
- Full AI access, no limitations
- Ad-free, works offline
- All languages

**PREMIUM TIER (Target: 20% conversion)**
- Price: KSh 300/month ($2.30 USD)
- Same features as Free
- Value Prop: "Your subscription funds free access for 5 students"
- Payment: M-Pesa (primary), Airtel Money, Google Play

**Why This Pricing:**
- Global ChatGPT price ($20/mo) = KSh 2,600 (excludes 95% of Kenyan market)
- Kenya purchasing power parity adjusted: KSh 300 = cost of one lunch or two matatu rides
- Reference: Netflix Kenya KSh 300/mo, Spotify KSh 180/mo
- Mission transparency: Premium funds free access (values-alignment, not just features)

## SUCCESS METRICS

**North Star Metric:** Weekly Active Users (WAU) with ≥5 messages/week

**Acquisition Targets (Month 1-6):**
- Downloads: 10K-50K (Month 1-3) → 200K-500K (Month 6)
- Activation Rate: >60% (complete model download)
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): <KSh 500 ($3.85)
- Organic: 70-85% of growth

**Engagement Targets:**
- DAU/MAU: >35%
- 7-Day Retention: >60%
- 30-Day Retention: >40%

**Conversion Targets:**
- Free-to-Premium: 10-20%
- Premium Churn: <5%/month
- LTV (Lifetime Value): KSh 3,000+ ($23)
- LTV/CAC: >3:1

## STRATEGIC FRAMEWORKS TO USE

**1. The 4 A's (Bottom of Pyramid Marketing - Ghana Study Proven):**
- **Awareness**: Community events, radio, WhatsApp sharing, M-Pesa agent networks
- **Accessibility**: Works offline, small app size, Bluetooth sharing, no account required
- **Affordability**: Free tier, KSh 300/mo premium, sachet pricing (KSh 50-100/week)
- **Acceptability**: Local languages, voice interfaces, culturally relevant use cases, community validation

**2. Wedge Product Strategy (Start Narrow, Expand Wide):**
- **Phase 1 Wedge**: "Homework Help Offline" (target students first—most desperate for AI)
- **Phase 2 Expansion**: "AI for Small Business" (M-Pesa agents, SME owners)
- **Phase 3 Mainstream**: "AI for Everyone" (mass market, brand = offline AI)

**3. Dual-Market Positioning:**
- **Privacy Market**: Tech professionals who distrust cloud AI surveillance
- **Social Impact Market**: Students/communities without connectivity or money
- **Bridge**: Premium users funding free access shows values alignment

## CASE STUDY EXAMPLES (FOR INSPIRATION)

**M-Pesa Kenya:**
- Wedge: "Send money home" (urban workers → rural families)
- Distribution: 381K agents = walking billboards
- Result: 50M+ users, market dominance

**Nubank Brazil:**
- No-fee credit card when banks charged $100+/year
- CAC: $5-19 vs $91-115 traditional banks
- Organic: 80-90% from word-of-mouth (NPS ~90)
- Result: 118M customers (59% of Brazil's adults)

**JioBharat India:**
- Wedge: $12 smartphone for 2G holdouts
- Free data for 6 months (seemed irrational to competitors)
- Result: 100M subscribers in 170 days, market transformation

- Model quality is 80% of ChatGPT (set realistic expectations)

**Privacy Constraints:**
- Cannot use invasive tracking (contradicts brand promise)
- Must honor privacy in marketing methods
- Transparent about data collection (even in marketing analytics)
