## Settlor Briefing Script

Tell your settlor:
> "I'm setting up a family trust as part of my corporate structure. I need a third party — someone with no financial interest in the trust — to establish it with a $10 contribution. You'll sign the trust deed, hand over $10, and that's your only involvement. You will never receive anything from the trust. If CRA ever asks, just explain honestly that I asked you to help set up the trust and you contributed $10."

**Key points for settlor to remember:**
1. They contributed $10 of their own money
2. They have NO interest in the trust
3. They will receive nothing from the trust
4. They did this as a favour / to help with business structuring
