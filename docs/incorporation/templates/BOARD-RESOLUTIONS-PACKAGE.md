### 2.10 QSBC COMPLIANCE MONITORING

**RESOLVED THAT:**

1. The Corporation implement quarterly monitoring of assets to ensure compliance with Qualified Small Business Corporation (QSBC) requirements for Lifetime Capital Gains Exemption (LCGE) eligibility.

2. The Treasurer shall prepare a quarterly asset report showing:
   a) Total active business assets (equipment, IP, working capital);
   b) Total passive assets (excess cash, investments);
   c) Active asset percentage (must exceed 50% throughout 24-month holding period and 90% at time of sale).

3. If active asset percentage falls below 60%, the Board shall take immediate action to purify the asset base by:
   a) Accelerating planned business spending;
   b) Paying dividends to holding company (if applicable);
   c) Repaying shareholder loans;
   d) Other appropriate measures.

### 2.11 CORPORATE POLICIES

**RESOLVED THAT:**

The following corporate policies be adopted:

**Related Party Transactions:**
