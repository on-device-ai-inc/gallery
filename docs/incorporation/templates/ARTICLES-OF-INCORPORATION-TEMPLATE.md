## CLASS A COMMON SHARES (Voting, Founder Control)

**Authorized:** Unlimited

**Rights and Restrictions:**

1. **Voting:** Each Class A Common Share entitles the holder to one (1) vote at all meetings of shareholders.

2. **Dividends:** The holders of Class A Common Shares shall be entitled to receive dividends as and when declared by the Board of Directors, pari passu with holders of Class B Common Shares.

3. **Liquidation:** Upon liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Corporation, the holders of Class A Common Shares shall be entitled to participate equally with holders of Class B Common Shares in the distribution of the remaining property and assets of the Corporation, after payment of all liabilities.

4. **Not Redeemable:** Class A Common Shares are not redeemable by the Corporation.

5. **Not Retractable:** Class A Common Shares are not retractable at the option of the holder.

**Purpose:** Maintain founder control through voting rights while enabling LCGE multiplication through non-voting Class B shares.

---

## CLASS B COMMON SHARES (Non-Voting, Family Trust)

**Authorized:** Unlimited

**Rights and Restrictions:**

1. **Voting:** Class B Common Shares are NON-VOTING and do not entitle the holder to vote at any meetings of shareholders, except as required by law.

   **Exception:** Holders of Class B Common Shares shall be entitled to vote separately as a class if the Corporation proposes to:
   - Amend the rights, privileges, restrictions, or conditions attached to Class B Common Shares
   - Create shares ranking senior to Class B Common Shares
   - Otherwise materially adversely affect the interests of Class B shareholders

2. **Dividends:** The holders of Class B Common Shares shall be entitled to receive dividends as and when declared by the Board of Directors, pari passu with holders of Class A Common Shares.

3. **Liquidation:** Upon liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Corporation, the holders of Class B Common Shares shall be entitled to participate equally with holders of Class A Common Shares in the distribution of the remaining property and assets of the Corporation, after payment of all liabilities.

4. **Not Redeemable:** Class B Common Shares are not redeemable by the Corporation.

5. **Not Retractable:** Class B Common Shares are not retractable at the option of the holder.

6. **Conversion:** Class B Common Shares may be converted into Class A Common Shares on a one-for-one basis at the option of the holder, subject to any restrictions on share transfers under applicable securities law and any shareholders' agreement.

**Purpose:** Held by Family Trust to enable each child beneficiary to claim individual LCGE on future sale. Non-voting preserves founder control.

## CLASS C PREFERRED SHARES (Investment, Future Use)

**Authorized:** Unlimited

**Rights and Restrictions:**

1. **Voting:** Class C Preferred Shares are NON-VOTING and do not entitle the holder to vote at any meetings of shareholders, except as required by law.

2. **Dividends:** The holders of Class C Preferred Shares shall be entitled to receive cumulative preferential dividends at an annual rate of [___]% of the issue price per share, as and when declared by the Board of Directors, in priority to any dividends on Class A Common Shares or Class B Common Shares.

   **Cumulative:** If dividends are not declared in any year, they shall accumulate and be payable before any dividends on Class A or Class B shares.

3. **Liquidation:** Upon liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Corporation, the holders of Class C Preferred Shares shall be entitled to receive, in priority to holders of Class A Common Shares and Class B Common Shares, an amount equal to the issue price per share plus all accrued and unpaid dividends.

4. **Redemption:** Class C Preferred Shares shall be redeemable by the Corporation at any time at the option of the Corporation upon payment of the issue price per share plus all accrued and unpaid dividends.

   **Notice:** The Corporation shall give at least 30 days' written notice of redemption to the holders.

5. **Retraction:** Class C Preferred Shares may be retracted at the option of the holder at any time upon giving 90 days' written notice to the Corporation. The Corporation shall pay the holder the issue price per share plus all accrued and unpaid dividends within 90 days of receipt of the notice.

   **Corporation's Option:** The Corporation may, within 30 days of receiving a retraction notice, elect instead to convert the Class C Preferred Shares into Class A Common Shares at a conversion ratio to be determined by the Board of Directors.

6. **Conversion:** Class C Preferred Shares may be converted into Class A Common Shares at the option of the holder at a conversion ratio to be determined by the Board of Directors at the time of issuance.

**Purpose:** Reserved for future investors. Allows raising capital without diluting founder voting control. Preferred terms protect investors while preserving QSBC qualification.

**Note:** None issued initially. Available for future financing rounds.

## CLASS D GROWTH SHARES (Future LCGE Allocation)

**Authorized:** Unlimited

**Rights and Restrictions:**

1. **Voting:** Class D Growth Shares are NON-VOTING and do not entitle the holder to vote at any meetings of shareholders, except as required by law.

2. **Dividends:** The holders of Class D Growth Shares shall NOT be entitled to receive dividends.

3. **Liquidation:** Upon liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Corporation, the holders of Class D Growth Shares shall be entitled to receive, after payment to holders of Class C Preferred Shares but pari passu with holders of Class A and Class B Common Shares, an amount equal to the fair market value of the Corporation's assets at the time of liquidation, less all liabilities and amounts owing to Class C Preferred shareholders.

   **Growth Participation:** Class D Growth Shares participate only in appreciation above a specified threshold amount to be determined by the Board of Directors at the time of issuance.

4. **Not Redeemable:** Class D Growth Shares are not redeemable by the Corporation.

5. **Not Retractable:** Class D Growth Shares are not retractable at the option of the holder.

6. **Conversion:** Class D Growth Shares may be converted into Class A Common Shares or Class B Common Shares on terms to be determined by the Board of Directors at the time of issuance.

**Purpose:** Future flexibility for additional LCGE multiplication or employee/advisor equity incentives. Participates in growth only, not existing value.

**Note:** None issued initially. Available for estate freezes or additional family members.
