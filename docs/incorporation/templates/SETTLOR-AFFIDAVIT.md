- Trust Property is held subject to my direction or control

- I have no beneficial interest in the Trust
- Trust Property cannot revert to me
- I have no control over the Trust
- Subsection 75(2) does NOT apply

**7.3** I understand that if subsection 75(2) applies due to my actions or failure to maintain independence, the tax planning benefits of this Trust could be destroyed, resulting in potential tax liabilities of **$1.8 million to $2.5 million or more**.

**7.4** I therefore commit to:
