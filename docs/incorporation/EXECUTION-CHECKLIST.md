## PHASE 3: TRUST EXECUTION & SHARE ISSUANCE (Week 3)

### Task 9: Execute Trust Deed

**What to do:**

**CRITICAL:** Only execute AFTER lawyer has reviewed and corrected the deed!

1. **Receive corrected trust deed from lawyer**

2. **Print 3 copies** (original for trust, copy for minute book, copy for trustees)

3. **Arrange signing:**
   - Settlor (you) signs in presence of witness
   - Trustees (you and/or spouse) each sign in presence of witness
   - Witnesses must be 18+, not beneficiaries, not each other

4. **Witness requirements:**
   - Must be present when you sign
   - Must sign their name, print name, address, occupation
   - Should NOT be spouse or children (avoid conflict)
   - Consider: friend, neighbour, colleague, notary

5. **Complete Schedule A:**
   - Settlor transfers $10 to Trustees
   - Trustees acknowledge receipt
   - Sign and date

6. **Store:**
   - Original: Secure location (safe, safety deposit box)
   - Copy: Corporate minute book
   - Copy: Trustees' records

**Result:** Family Trust is now validly established
